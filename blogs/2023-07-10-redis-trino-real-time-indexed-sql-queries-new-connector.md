Ever since the pandemic, it has become clear that the need for a digital first economy is becoming more and more necessary. As Redis’ Field CTO Allen Terleto said during their talk from Trino Fest 2023, “In a digital first economy, data is the lifeblood of the organization, which makes the databases the heart of enterprise architectures”. Redis, a popular open source project, is a distributed in-memory key–value database.
