Trino is deployed everywhere – on-premise, in private data centers, in the cloud with hosting providers, on bare metal servers, on virtual machines, and with containers. With all these options for deployments, a Kubernetes-based platform with a container emerged as the most widely used approach. The Trino project caters for this usage with our container images for every release and our Helm chart.
