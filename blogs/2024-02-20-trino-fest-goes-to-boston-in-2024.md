After the resounding success of Trino Fest and Trino Summit in 2023, Commander Bun Bun has exciting news to share: we’re taking our biggest events of the year back to being in-person. They’ll be hybrid, to be more specific, so if you can’t travel, don’t fret, you’ll still be able to watch and ask questions in chat. But if you can travel, you won’t want to miss out!
