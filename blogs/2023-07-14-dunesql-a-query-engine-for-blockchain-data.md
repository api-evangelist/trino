The need to make blockchain data easily accessible has risen over the recent years due to the popularity of cryptocurrencies, NFTs, and other uses of blockchains. Dune has made it their mission to make blockchain data more accessible. Dune is a community data platform for querying public blockchain data and building beautiful dashboards.
