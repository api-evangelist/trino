<h2 id="keynote">Keynote</h2>

<p>In the keynote <strong>Enduring with persistence to reach the summit</strong> Martin
Traverso, co-creator of Trino and CTO at
<a href="https://trino.io/users.html#starburst">Starburst</a>, covers the developments from
2024 in the Trino projects and the Trino community. Martin also reveals details
about new features, new projects, and plans for 2025.</p>

<h2 id="panel-discussion">Panel discussion</h2>

<p>The hype and reality of AI has swept through the industry. In the panel
discussion <strong>Lessons and news from the AI world for Trino</strong>, Manfred Moser is
moderating experts from the community:</p>

<ul>
  <li>Gunther Hagleitner, CEO and Co-founder at <a href="https://waii.ai/">Waii</a></li>
  <li>Rong Rong, Software Engineer at <a href="https://character.ai/">CharacterAI</a></li>
  <li>William Chang, Co-founder and CTO of <a href="https://trino.io/users.html#canner">Canner</a> and
<a href="https://trino.io/ecosystem/client#wren-ai">WrenAI</a></li>
  <li>Mustafa Sakalsiz, Founder and CEO at <a href="https://trino.io/users.html#peaka">Peaka</a></li>
  <li>Dain Sundstrom, Trino co-creator and CTO at <a href="https://trino.io/users.html#starburst">Starburst</a></li>
</ul>

<p>All panelists have have extensive experience with AI and Trino, and will share
their knowledge and different perspectives.</p>
