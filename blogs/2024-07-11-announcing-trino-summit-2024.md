Fresh off the heels of Trino Fest 2024, where Commander Bun Bun was busy meeting the Trino community in-person, we’re already looking forward to another, bigger event to round out the year in Trino. For those who’ve been here a while, you know that can only mean one thing: Trino Summit 2024. Much like last year, it will be a two-day, fully virtual event, hosting a wide range of talks covering all things Trino on the 11th and 12th of December.
