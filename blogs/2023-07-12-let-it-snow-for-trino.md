In this recap, we can skip right to the exciting part: through the joint efforts of engineers at ForePaaS and Bloomberg, there is a Snowflake connector coming to Trino! Though it hasn’t landed yet, it has been tested and run in production at both companies, and a pull request is open and working its way towards completion as this blog post goes up. In the talk, Yu and Erik talk about difficulties in developing the connector, the motivations to make it happen, and the new features that come as part of it for Trino users to take advantage of.
